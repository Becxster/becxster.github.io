Website Development

In 2024, my professors, Dr. Mikki Hebl and Dr. Eden King, released a book called Working Together , a book dedicated to uncovering the links between their Industrial Organizational Psychology research and the importance of diversity in the workplace. I took charge in curating a website for their book on my own, completely from scratch.

Workingtogetherbook.com was coded from the bottom up using solely HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which allowed me various creative liberties that website builders like Weebly and Google Sites do not. Furthermore, I learned a lot about the technicals of web design in the process and my project lowered the upkeep cost of their website by 300% annually!
